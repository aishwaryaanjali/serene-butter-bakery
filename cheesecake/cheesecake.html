CHEESECAKE

Ingredients:

  • Graham crackers (for crust)
  • Butter (melted)
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sour cream or heavy cream
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Preparation:

  • Preheat oven to 325ºF (160ºC). Line a springform pan with parchment paper.
  • Mix crushed graham crackers and melted butter. Press into pan to form crust.
  • Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, eggs (one at a time), vanilla, and sour cream. Mix until combined.
  • Pour batter over crust. Tap to remove bubbles.
  • Bake for 50–60 minutes. The center should be slightly jiggly.
  • Turn off oven, crack door, and let cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  • Chill in fridge for 4+ hours or overnight. Add toppings like fruit or chocolate before serving.
