CROISSANT

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Instant yeast
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Unsalted butter (for dough and laminating)
  • Egg (for egg wash)

Preparation:

  • Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add milk and water, and knead into a soft dough. Chill for 1 hour.
  • Roll dough into a rectangle. Place chilled butter in the center, fold, and roll out again.
  • Perform 3 folds (letter-style), chilling 30 mins between each.
  • Roll out and cut triangles. Roll each from base to tip to form croissants.
  • Place on a tray, cover, and let proof for 1–2 hours.
  • Brush with egg wash. Bake at 375ºF (190ºC) for 20–25 minutes until golden and flaky.
  • Cool slightly before serving. Best enjoyed warm!
