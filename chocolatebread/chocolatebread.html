CHOCOLATE BREAD

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Granulated sugar
  • Salt
  • Instant yeast
  • Warm milk
  • Egg
  • Unsalted butter
  • Chocolate chips or chunks

Preparation:

  • In a bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let sit until foamy (about 5–10 mins).
  • In a large bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  • Add egg and yeast mixture to the dry ingredients. Knead until combined.
  • Gradually add butter and knead until smooth and elastic (10–15 minutes).
  • Fold in chocolate chips. Cover and let rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour).
  • Punch down and shape into a loaf or braid. Let rise again for 30–45 minutes.
  • Preheat oven to 350ºF (175ºC). Bake for 30–35 minutes, until the top is firm and a skewer comes out mostly clean.
  • Cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.
