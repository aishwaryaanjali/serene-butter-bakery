CUPCAKE

ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Vinegar

Preparation:

  • Preheat the oven to 350º F (180º C). Line the cupcake pans with liners. Measure all the ingredients.
  • Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
  • Combine milk and vinegar in a small bowl
  • In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter until creamy and pale. Add vanilla and mix to combine
  • Add the flour alternately with the milk mixture, beginning and ending with the flour mixture; beat until smooth.
  • Divide batter evenly among liners, about three-quarters full each. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let them cool in the pan for a couple of minutes, then remove. Allow cooling completely before frosting.
  • Using an electric hand mixer or a stand mixer on medium speed, beat in a large bowl the unsalted butter until smooth, pale and creamy, about 8 minutes.
  • Reduce mixer speed to low. Add half of the confectioners’ sugar, and keep mixing until incorporated
  • Add the remaining confectioners’ sugar, vanilla, and salt. Once incorporated, beat on low speed for 1 minute, then increase the speed to medium-high and beat for 4 – 5 minutes until the frosting is smooth, fluffy, and spreadable, scraping down the bowl once or twice.
  • Add the liquid (milk, heavy cream, or water) to adjust the consistency of the buttercream. It should be smooth and spreadable. Depending on how warm or cold your butter was at the beginning and your kitchen temperature, you will need to add 1 -2 tablespoons of liquid. In hot weather, I find myself not adding any liquid at all.
  • Put it in a piping bag and swirl it around on the cupcake and serve
