CHOCOLATE LAVA CAKE

Ingredients:

  • Semi-sweet chocolate
  • Unsalted butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt

Preparation:

  • Preheat oven to 425ºF (220ºC). Butter and flour ramekins.
  • Melt chocolate and butter together until smooth.
  • In a bowl, beat eggs and sugar until thick and pale. Add vanilla and salt.
  • Gently mix in melted chocolate, then fold in flour until just combined.
  • Pour into ramekins, filling about ¾ full.
  • Bake for 12–14 minutes until the edges are firm but center is still soft.
  • Let rest for 1 minute, invert onto plates, and serve warm. Optionally dust with sugar or serve with ice cream.
