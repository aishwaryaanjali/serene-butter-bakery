DONUT

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Powdered sugar / glaze / chocolate (for topping)

Preparation:

  • Activate yeast in warm milk and sugar. Let it foam.
  • Mix flour, salt, eggs, butter, and yeast mixture. Knead into soft dough.
  • Let rise in a warm place until doubled (about 1 hour).
  • Roll out dough and cut into rings. Let rise again for 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil to 350ºF (175ºC). Fry donuts until golden brown on each side.
  • Drain on paper towels. Dip in glaze, chocolate, or sprinkle powdered sugar while warm.
